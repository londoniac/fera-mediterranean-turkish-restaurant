FERA

A taste of the Mediterranean in East Grinstead.

At Fera, we bring the rich traditions of Mediterranean and Turkish cooking to your table. Every dish is prepared with care using fresh, quality ingredients, from chargrilled kebabs and mezze platters to fragrant rice dishes and homemade bread straight from the oven.

Whether you're joining us for a relaxed dinner, a family celebration, or a quick lunch, you'll find generous portions, genuine flavours, and a warm welcome every time.
