A taste of the Mediterranean in East Grinstead.

At Fera, we bring the rich traditions of Mediterranean and Turkish cooking to your table. Every dish is prepared with care using fresh, quality ingredients — from chargrilled kebabs and mezze platters to fragrant rice dishes and homemade bread.

Whether you're joining us for a relaxed dinner, a family celebration, or a quick lunch, you'll find generous portions, genuine flavours, and a warm welcome every time.

From our kitchen

Mezze Platter

A generous selection of hummus, baba ganoush, stuffed vine leaves, falafel, and warm flatbread.

Chargrilled Kebabs

Tender lamb, chicken, or mixed grill kebabs, cooked over charcoal and served with rice and salad.

Pide & Lahmacun

Traditional Turkish flatbreads baked to order with your choice of toppings — meat, cheese, or vegetable.

Fresh Fish

Sea bass or sea bream, grilled whole and served with Mediterranean vegetables and lemon butter.

Vegetarian Specials

Stuffed aubergine, spinach börek, and falafel wraps — packed with flavour, no meat needed.

Turkish Desserts

Baklava, künefe, and Turkish tea to round off your meal — sweet, flaky, and utterly indulgent.
